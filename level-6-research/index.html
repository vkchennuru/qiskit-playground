üî¨ Design a Quantum Solution

Choose one of these real-world problems and design a quantum computing approach to solve it:

Option 1: Drug Discovery

Design a VQE circuit to calculate the ground state energy of a simple molecule (H‚ÇÇ or LiH). Explain which gates you'll use, why, and how you'll handle noise on real hardware.

Option 2: Optimization Problem

Create a QAOA circuit to solve a small traveling salesman problem (4 cities). Show your circuit design, explain the mixer and cost Hamiltonians, and estimate the quantum advantage.

Option 3: Quantum Search

Implement Grover's algorithm to search a database of 16 items. Calculate the number of iterations needed, design the oracle, and compare with classical search.

Option 4: Secure Communication

Design a BB84 quantum key distribution protocol for two parties. Explain how eavesdropping would be detected and why it's impossible to break.
